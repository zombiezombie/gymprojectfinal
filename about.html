MEET THE GLADIATOR COMMANDER



Robert "Gunny" Barker




Gladiator Fitness is owned and operated by Robert "Gunny" Barker, a former U. S. Marine, martial artist,

and nationally certified fitness professional. He brings over 25 years experience in training Marines, military

personnel, and civilians to every workout.


One of the reasons why I started Gladiator Fitness is because I am absolutely passionate about helping

others reach levels of fitness they never dreamed possible. Another reason is because I was fed up with the

amount of misleading advertising and information we are subjected to when it comes to exercise and

nutrition, and what it really takes to create a lean, strong, and healthy body.


Let's face it, the food and fitness industry doesn't give a hoot whether you ever get fit or not, so long as you

keep buying their products. Every time you turn around, someone is trying to sell you some magic pill or

new workout program that is going to magically melt fat away and get you looking like a supermodel in just

14 days! I'm here to tell you that creating a lean, strong, and healthy body takes time, commitment, and hard

work -- but it absolutely can be done if you commit yourself to eating right and exercising. Let me help you

get started today!
